Hello everyone, I am an incoming CAS student and am wondering about the dorms at BU. I am thinking of staying at warrens or west. Can someone give me a rough idea of the pros and cons of each dorm as well as your preference?

Warren and West are great first year dorms. Very standard size rooms for doubles and triples. Warren quads are a tight squeeze though.
Warren has common rooms on each floor but West doesn't. I lived in Rich in West which has the largest lobby and study spaces if the 3 West campus dorms.

Towers is also a common first year dorm, but only has single sex floors. I don't think there are common rooms on each floor if I remember correctly. But lots of common and study spaces on the first floor.

Location wise: West is great for CGS, SHA, and CFA. But not too far a walk from CAS, plus CAS classes tend to be scheduled all over campus. Warren and Tower are both great for SAR, QST, and COM.

Warren, Claflin and Sleeper (two of the West dorms) all have the benefit of not having to leave the building to go to the dining hall.

Avoid Danielson like the plauge! It's so far from everything else.
