It fully depends on what youre looking for however I've seen a lot of freshmen complain about not even being in their top few choices.

Warren --> A tower will be renovated, nice with AC, however will probably be pricey, along with west the top dorm in terms of socialization, has dining hall inside

West --> from what I've heard better than warren, however likely removed from classes a bit (Unless SHA or CFA), dining hall a short walk, close to the gym if that matters for you

Towers --> Less likely but possible placement, Short walk to dining hall, don't know a lot but has sophomores and juniors so less of a freshmen sociable dorm

Bay state --> If you'd like you can try your luck, I got a nice baystate freshmen year and stayed there until I was able to go to an apt. There were actually a lot of freshmen in my building, you'll have to walk to the dining hall but nicer dorms/bigger, however know it is very unlikely

Fenway --> BEST dining hall, currently a freshmen dorm (idk if its gonna stay that way), however secluded from campus where you'll either have to take a decent walk or take the bus. The dorms are decent from what I've heard but it depends where in fenway

So I live in Fenway rn and I will say I DO NOT reccommend. It can be very lonely and actually hinder you from making friends. The area however is really nice. It does feel more like traditional campus living and its honestly rly cute. Riverway is supposed to be the best house in fenway with spacious rooms, gender neutral bathrooms only (basically private bc its for one person at a time), ac, and shared kitchen. I've heard Pilgrim house in Fenway has cockroaches in the warmer months and none of the other houses on campus have ac if i am not mistaken. Overall, there are pros and cons. I've had a lot of trouble socially at BU and I attribute it ALL to the location. If I could do it over I would go for either Bay State or West because although the "commute" may seem bad, there is literally the t running straight down commonwealth and the BU bus is awesome (dont listen to the hate, you just have to learn how it works). You're gonna be walking alot regardless and Fenway is as far (if not further) to most classes than the dorms on main campus (fenwayers dont consider fenway to be main campus). Overall, it is nice that Fenway is freshman only and it is more than bearable, I just wouldn't do it ever again. To each their own.
