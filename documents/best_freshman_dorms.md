The brownstones have a good location for CAS and they are generally very spacious doubles (some triples and singles). Unlikely but possible if it's your first choice. However, some feel painfully outdated.

The Towers and 575 Comm Ave are both really outdated but in great spots imo. Quieter than the bigger buildings.

Kilachand is a bit nicer, with central air conditioning and sizable rooms (generally 5 person suites made of a double, a triple and a private bathroom), but it's another block farther than towers and 575.

Don't bother with West if you want a location near CAS.

You could just pick Warren and just pray you get the renovated tower (it's gonna be like 50/50).
